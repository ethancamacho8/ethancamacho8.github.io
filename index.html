Background
Ever since I was little, being active and involved in sports has been at the center of my life. I have seen firsthand the transformative power of sport, and hope to bring more people into this space through user-centered design. Emphasizing safety and comfort, I am committed to developing innovative products that combine biomechanics, anatomy, research, and design to give people of all backgrounds the confidence to move freely and without fear.
